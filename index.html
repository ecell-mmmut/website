About Us

MMMUT (formerly Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur) was established in 1962 with the primary motive to impart quality education and train the youth to take up the future challenges of evolving India. The first and only residential technical University in U.P. with a total strength of 7000 students is presently running seven B.Tech. programs along with M.B.A., M.C.A., M.Tech. in 12 disciplines and fulltime Ph.D. programme in number of disciplines as part of Quality Improvement Programme of MHRD and TEQIP-II project. MMMUT has become a leading institution in engineering and management with departments as centers of excellence, imparting high quality education and providing ambience for growth of professionals as excellent human beings as well.

Entrepreneurship Cell is students society of MMMUT which focuses primarily to promote entrepreneurial abilities among students. To accomplish this periodic guest lectures, expert talks, workshops and various events of the domain are frequently organized by the cell. Entrepreneurship Cell creates a network of students, alumni, teachers and entrepreneurs together to help out any startup being raised by a student anyway.

Mission

As Ecell, MMMUT ,we believe in adding values to individuals by bringing young thoughts into action. Entrepreneurs fired with ideas embody the true passion for growing, we try to achieve that through nurturing the ideas of advancing innovation with compassion and enhancing productivity.We are incorporating some interventions in this direction to strengthen our multifarious programmers.

Vision

We aim at nurturing the passion for self employment that will enable a critical drive for innovation that incorporates edifying the entrepreneurial ecosystem and nourishing team building skills. Besides, Analyzing pilot feasibility and providing a detailed methodology that will foster the startup and self-employment culture among aspiring technopreneurs. We aim to continue our effort in the direction of creating enterprising youth who are capable of surviving in an entrepreneurial society.

Values

We at E-Cell MMMUT, believe in high professional ethics and human values, and emphasize on their practice in our operations. Our core values are-Pursuit of excellence, learning by doing, Transparency, Integrity, Mutual Respect.
